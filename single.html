Assume it’s true so why haven’t we made contact with them yet? My say is that if an alien is looking at us from 60 or so Million Light Years away he is probably looking at dinosaurs (Mesozoic Era) right now.

Why?

Because that's how light works or I should say that’s how our sight works the images we see are made up of light reflected from the objects we look at. Now that’s out of the way. Light Years are a measuring unit of distance/length not time (Point to be noted). The light-year is a unit of length used to express astronomical distances and measures about 9.46 trillion kilometers or 5.88 trillion miles meaning it’ll take light one year to reach something that is about 9.46 trillion kilometers (it takes sunlight an average of 8 minutes and 20 seconds to travel from the Sun to the Earth which is only 149.6 million km away. Not much I would say :p ) back to Mesozoic Era that’s the reason we haven’t made any contacts yet or they have made contact with us.
